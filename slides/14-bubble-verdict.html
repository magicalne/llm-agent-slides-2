So... Is It a Bubble?

Two things can be true at the same time.

✓ The Tech Is Real

The underlying technology is real and still improving

Models are getting better, cheaper, more capable

Real products are being built and used daily

✗ The Financial Layer Is Not

Circular deals inflate revenue on paper

Stock swaps disguise the true cost of growth

Space data centers and "abundant intelligence" are narratives, not products

We are still hiking — but there IS a bubble in the financial layer.
