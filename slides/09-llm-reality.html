Criticism & Nuance

What LLMs Actually Are (and Aren't)

The Criticism

Requires enormous amounts of data

Pattern-matching on human output, not "thinking"

No real understanding behind the text

The Counterpoint (Ilya)

LLMs encode relational structure of the world in weights

"Red" is closer to "purple" than "yellow" — without ever seeing color

Something meaningful IS captured — just not intelligence as we mean it

LLMs are not nothing. But they're also not what the market is pricing them as.

Hallucination

Confident Pattern Completers, Not Reasoners

Hallucination isn't a bug to fix — it reveals what LLMs fundamentally are.

Standard Explanation

Insufficient training data, underfitting

More data + more compute = less hallucination

Deeper Take

LLMs don't know what they don't know

They complete patterns with confidence, even when wrong

"Can a human kill another human?"
